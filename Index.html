A REVOLUTIONARY TRADITIONAL APPAREL BRAND

We are OGNOP STITCH4U PRIVATE LIMITED, a clothing brand born in Kolkata, dedicated to merging the timeless elegance of the kurta with contemporary newness and customization. If anyone thinks about innovation and kurtas, they should think about us.

OUR AUDIENCE: FOCUSED & FORWARD-THINKING

Our primary market is the dynamic, trend-aware 25-30 year old demographic. Crucially, we recognize the significant role of women in the purchasing process for men's apparel, and we design our platform and products to appeal directly to the discerning tastes of these shoppers.

B2B OPERATIONS (CURRENT)

We currently operate as trusted B2B resellers of high-quality designer kurtas, leveraging our Kolkata base to source and supply exclusive apparel to businesses. This foundation ensures deep market knowledge and robust quality control, which we carry forward into our future B2C model.

THE FUTURE IS CUSTOM: KHADI DESIGN STUDIO

DESIGN YOUR OWN SHORT KURTA

Our forthcoming B2C model will allow customers to step into our Design Studio and create their perfect short kurta using our sustainable Khadi fabric base. Customers select every detail from a comprehensive digital catalogue, ensuring a truly personalized garment delivered right to their doorstep.

TRANSPARENT MODULAR PRICING

To move away from seasonal business models, we offer clear, non-seasonal pricing. There will be a definitive base price for a plain Khadi short kurta, and customers will add to this base price by selecting specific designer elements from our catalogue, allowing for budgetary control and design flexibility.

THE POWER OF KHADI

We believe Khadi embodies the true spirit of Indian tradition while being versatile enough for modern silhouettes. Our focus on this resilient fabric ensures a high-quality, comfortable, and conscious product that stands the test of time, both in style and durability.

CONNECT WITH THE FUTURE OF APPAREL

We are actively building our B2C Design Studio and are seeking B2B partnerships. Contact us today to discuss your designer kurta requirements or to partner with us on our revolutionary Khadi customization journey.
